About Pogiso's Tours

Founded in 2015, Pogiso's Tours emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to redefine luxury travel in South Africa. What began as a boutique chauffeur service with just five vehicles has evolved into a comprehensive premium travel experience platform serving thousands of discerning clients annually. Our founder, Pogiso Mthembu, recognized a gap in the market for truly personalized, reliable, and elegant transportation solutions. Today, we operate a fleet of over 200 meticulously maintained vehicles and employ more than 150 professionally trained chauffeurs, all united by our unwavering commitment to excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our luxury chauffeur services and premium travel packages.

We recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance to ensure availability of our premium vehicles and experienced chauffeurs. However, we understand that travel plans can change unexpectedly. Our concierge team works diligently to accommodate last-minute requests, often fulfilling same-day bookings within 2-3 hours. For special events, international delegations, or large group bookings, we suggest booking 1-2 weeks in advance to allow our team to customize the perfect experience tailored to your specific requirements and preferences.

Our comprehensive luxury packages include professional chauffeur service, premium vehicle selection from our fleet, complimentary refreshments (water, juices, coffee), Wi-Fi connectivity for business travelers, phone charging facilities, newspapers and magazines, and 24/7 customer support. Depending on your chosen package tier, you may also receive concierge services, airport meet-and-greet, luggage assistance, travel insurance, and personalized itinerary planning. Each package is customizable to meet your specific needs and preferences.

Yes, our e-vouchers are fully transferable, making them perfect for gifting to friends, family, or business associates. Vouchers are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase, providing plenty of flexibility for the recipient to schedule their luxury travel experience at their convenience. We offer complimentary voucher extensions upon request, and recipients can easily redeem their vouchers through our user-friendly online platform or by contacting our dedicated concierge team. No blackout dates apply to our premium packages, ensuring maximum flexibility and accessibility.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, mobile money services, and digital wallets. Your payment information is protected by industry-leading 256-bit SSL encryption and processed through PCI DSS-compliant payment gateways. We never store sensitive card details on our servers, and all transactions are monitored for fraud protection. Your privacy is paramount—we comply with international data protection regulations and never share your information with third parties without explicit consent.

We offer flexible cancellation terms with full refunds for cancellations made 72 hours or more before your scheduled service. Cancellations within 72 hours incur a 50% service fee, while cancellations within 24 hours may forfeit the entire booking amount. However, we understand that circumstances change—contact our concierge team to discuss your situation, and we'll work with you to find the best solution. Modifications to existing bookings (date, time, or vehicle type) can be made free of charge up to 48 hours before service, subject to availability.
